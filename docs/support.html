SheetSweep Support

Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats does SheetSweep support?

SheetSweep supports .xlsx (Microsoft Excel) and .csv (Comma-Separated Values) files.

Is my data sent to a server?

No. All processing happens locally on your device. Your data never leaves your iPhone or iPad.

What types of issues can SheetSweep detect?

SheetSweep can detect: duplicate rows, inconsistent date formats, mixed currencies, extra spaces, hidden characters, missing values, and inconsistent column names.

How does the free tier work?

You can clean up to 3 files per month for free. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited cleaning and advanced features.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage your subscription in Settings > Your Name > Subscriptions on your iPhone, or in the App Store app.
