Privacy Policy for SheetSweep

Last updated: May 3, 2026

Data Collection

SheetSweep does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All spreadsheet processing happens entirely on your device. No data is sent to any server.

File Data

All files you import into SheetSweep are processed locally on your device. The app never uploads your files or their contents to any server. Processed data remains on your device and is deleted when you remove it from the app.

Analytics

SheetSweep does not use any analytics services, tracking tools, or third-party SDKs that collect user data.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your full payment information. Apple may share limited transaction data with us for the purpose of fulfilling your purchase.

Contact Support

When you use the Contact Support feature, the information you voluntarily provide (name, email, message) is sent to our support system. This data is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is not shared with third parties.

Children's Privacy

SheetSweep is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
